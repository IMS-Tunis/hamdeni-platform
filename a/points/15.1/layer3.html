How to complete this layer

  • Upload your first attempt with clear handwriting and clear separation between questions.
  • Check the Cambridge or Hodder mark scheme, then correct anything missing or wrong in a different color.
  • Upload your corrected work after reviewing the mark scheme.
  • Use the submission folder button below to open your personal folder and upload all four files listed.

File naming rules

Each file name must follow this pattern:

  • Point_Resource_Attempt

For example: 15.1_Cambridge_FirstAttempt

Files you must upload

Cambridge

15.1_Cambridge_FirstAttempt
15.1_Cambridge_AfterCorrection

Hodder

15.1_Hodder_FirstAttempt
15.1_Hodder_AfterCorrection

Make sure the point number, resource (Cambridge/Hodder), and attempt (FirstAttempt/AfterCorrection) are all visible in the file name.

Important: The folder link is personal. Do not share it with other students.

Activity 1: Cambridge end of chapter questions

Answer the Cambridge end of chapter questions for point 15.1 in your notebook or on paper.
Then scan or photograph your work and upload it.

  • Use clear handwriting and number your questions.
  • Keep the file size reasonable and make sure the image is readable.

Step 1: Upload your first attempt as 15.1_Cambridge_FirstAttempt.

Step 2: Open the Cambridge mark scheme PDF provided in the same folder as this page and correct your answers using a different color.

Step 3: Upload your corrected work as 15.1_Cambridge_AfterCorrection.

Activity 2: Hodder end of chapter questions

Answer the Hodder end of chapter questions for point 15.1 in your notebook or on paper, then upload your work.

  • Write the answers clearly and separate each question.
  • Keep the file size reasonable and ensure all pages are visible.

Step 1: Upload your first attempt as 15.1_Hodder_FirstAttempt.

Step 2: Open the Hodder mark scheme PDF provided in the same folder as this page and correct your answers using a different color.

Step 3: Upload your corrected work as 15.1_Hodder_AfterCorrection.
